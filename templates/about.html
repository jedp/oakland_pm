About this site

Oakland.PM is a versatile, web-based application also accessible via mobile devices that connects Oakland youth to after-school programs and enrichment opportunities.

Oakland offers many opportunities for after-school enrichment, but there is presently no on-line method for students to discover them according to their interests, their location, or their friendships. Our application will leverage the social media that youth are already using, such as facebook, twitter, and tumblr, to bring current and personalized information to them.

This site was created as part of the codeforoakland project. The team, led by Jed Parsons, was awarded the ‘Work with Oakland Prize’ by the City of Oakland. Contributors: Jed Parsons Kristi Holohan, Sonja Totten-Harris, Telly Koosis, Joshua Goldenberg, Christopher Yap, Dave Meyer, Aditya Pai, Greg Martin, Raimundo Martinez, Andrew Hao.

Privacy

This site has the utmost respect for privacy. We do not reveal personal information of any kind without express permission to do so from our users. Parts of the system connect to facebook, and users can elect to share certain elements of their profiles, class attendance, things they like, etc. The default setting is that everything is private, and sharing is completely optional, and easily revokable.
